## What we're building

We build software where **privacy and trust are architecture, not policy**.

Most products ask you to take their word for it — "we take your privacy
seriously," "your data is safe with us." We'd rather not ask you to trust us at
all. Our systems are designed so that we _can't_ read your data, _can't_ act
without your authority, and _can't_ hide what they did.

Three principles run through everything we ship:

- **Zero-knowledge by design** — encryption happens client-side. Ciphertext is
  all that ever leaves the device. No backdoor, no master key, nothing for us to
  lose or be compelled to hand over.
- **Human-held authority** — software acts under explicit human command, never on
  its own. The line between a copilot and an autopilot is one we won't cross.
- **Auditable by default** — every meaningful action is recorded in an
  append-only, replayable log. Trust you can verify, not just claim.

We work across **iOS, Android, and web**, with cross-platform cryptographic
consistency and the same threat model everywhere.

## Get started

Public repositories live right here on this org page. Most projects are in active
development — clone what's open, read the docs, and run the demos where available.

```bash
# Browse our open repositories
gh repo list ellosoft

# Clone one to take a look
gh repo clone ellosoft/<project>
```

Each repo ships its own README with setup, architecture notes, and the threat
model where it applies. Issues and discussions are open — that's the fastest way
to reach us.
